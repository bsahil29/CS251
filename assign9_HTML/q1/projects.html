Project : Adaptive Frequency Estimation Using Iterative DESA with RDFT Based Filter

Combined Discrete Energy Separation Algorithm (DESA-1) and Recursive Discrete Fourier Transform (RDFT)-Based Filter to design a new technique for Grid Voltage Frequency Estimation.
Proposed technique is computationally efficient and also robust to negative effects caused by noise and harmonics.
Learnt about DFT, FFT, DESA-1, DESA-1a, DESA-2, Teager Energy Operator (TEO) and many other techniques involved in Instantaneous Frequency Estimation.

Publication : Adaptive Frequency Estimation Using Iterative DESA with RDFT Based Filter

Proposed a method for estimating grid voltage fundamental frequency using a discrete-time energy separation algorithm (DESA) with a frequency adaptive band-pass filter (BPF).
Paper accepted for IEEE PES Asia-Pacific Power and Energy Engineering Conference, 2017.
